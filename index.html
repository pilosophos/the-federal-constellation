About the Constellation

The Federal Constellation (or simply, the Constellation) is a supranational union consisting of the founding members, the Celestial Imperium and the Great Equatorial Republic, as well as the Constellation Colonial Administration. It is headquartered in the Constellation Supersystem, containing the only two known habitable binary star systems joined by a natural wormhole.

The founding members have had a long-standing friendship since Communion Day via radio communication through the mutual wormhole, Everlasting Communion. On that day, the Friendship Compact was ratified, the precursor to the modern Constellation. Bravely advancing to the future, the two worlds developed the technology to colonise worlds outside the Constellation Supersystem, leading to the formation of the Federal Constellation on Constellation Day to better handle the needs of a growing alliance.
